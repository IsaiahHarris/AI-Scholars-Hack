Purpose
This app utilizes Amazon's Echo Dot as a voice-activated timer that incorporates the previously stated techniques to assist in task-production.
The Pomodoro Technique
The pomodoro technique revolves around working in 25 minute intervals with breaks in-between. This allows for a better flow and focus on your tasks.
The 20-20-20 Rule
The 20-20-20 rule is technique for people working on digital devices. In order to preserve and promote healthy eye functionality in later years of life, one should take a break every 20 minutes, and stare at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds.
